About Tracy

While I was in the Army in the early 2000's, I spent my spare time teaching myself HTML for fun. Over the course of the next several years, that learning blossomed into CSS and JavaScript.

I used that knowledge to manage a wonderful team of people to reface the website of a non-profit organization, and being around them made me want to start pursing web development as a career. I wanted to take my passion to the next level with Front End Development as my focus, but with Back End management in mind, so I joined up with the Thinkful Full Stack bootcamp to bolster my skill set.

I also spend a lot of time on YouTube and Reddit keeping up with new front end and UX/UI techniques and often work through classes on Udemy and StackSkills, as well as other learning sites.

I currently am specifically looking to work on a team of talented, imaginative programmers.

I've always been interested in financial markets and recently I have been learning about blockchain, cryptocurrency trading, and options trading and have been working on a few side projects to build web tools that can help people (and myself!) learn about and/or trade effectively and intelligently in these environments.

My other love is science, and to that effect I recieved a B.S. in Biological Sciences from University of Texas at Arlington. I hope to use the knowledge I gained there as I begin to learn and develop sophisticated Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence applications.

Outside of web coding, I enjoy my yearly camping trip, teaching others how to code, discovering new music, and working on writing a novel one day.

My hilarious little pug, Saki, is my coding co-pilot.
